About

Power systems
engineer & analyst.

I am a Principal Power Systems Engineer and Chartered Professional Engineer based in South East Queensland, currently with EPEC. My work sits at the intersection of modern power electronics and grid stability — particularly as Australia's energy mix shifts toward renewables.

I specialise in the end-to-end AEMO generator connection assessment process (R0–R2), and bring deep expertise in Electromagnetic Transient (EMT) modelling using PSCAD. I work with both solar PV and Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS), and have hands-on experience developing and validating complex inverter control models.

My focus is on technically rigorous, commercially grounded work — helping project developers navigate the NEM connection framework and de-risk their assets before energisation.
